# DASHBOARD
Swachh Sarvekshan Performance Dashboard (2016-2023)
Project Overview
This data analytics project provides a longitudinal analysis of the Swachh Sarvekshan, the annual cleanliness survey conducted across Indian cities. The dashboard serves as a strategic tool for visualizing the progress of the Swachh Bharat Mission (SBM), comparing city performance, and evaluating the impact of citizen feedback over an eight-year period.

Key Features & Visualizations
The dashboard is divided into several analytical modules to provide a 360-degree view of the data:

Geospatial Distribution: A map visualization identifying the concentration of surveyed cities across the Indian subcontinent.

Performance Metrics (KPIS):

Citizen Score: Highlighted at 20K, showing the aggregate engagement of the public.

Goal Tracking: A comparative analysis showing a massive increase in total scores between 2017 and 2022 (+47,281.92% growth).

Trend Analysis:

Score Evolution: Area and line charts showing fluctuations in performance by State and Year.

Cumulative Growth: A gauge chart tracking the "Sum of Citizen Feedback & Swachhata App" interactions, currently at 244K.

City Rankings & Comparison:

Top Performers (2023): Pie chart breakdown featuring leading cities like Indore, Surat, and Navi Mumbai.

Historical Breakdown: A stacked bar chart comparing city scores specifically for the years 2016, 2017, and 2018.

Treemap Analytics: Visual representation of the "Total Score" by city name for rapid identification of high-performing municipalities.

Technical Stack
Tool: Power BI / Microsoft Business Intelligence

Data Sources: Swachh Sarvekshan Annual Reports (2016-2023)

Visual Elements: Treemaps, Gauge Charts, Stacked Bar Charts, Pie Charts, and Map APIs.

Key Insights
Massive Scale-Up: The survey methodology and scoring criteria have expanded significantly, as evidenced by the "Total Score" goal tracking metric.

Citizen Centricity: A heavy emphasis on the "Swachhata App" data suggests that citizen feedback is a primary driver in modern city rankings.

Regional Leadership: The data consistently highlights competitive performance among major urban hubs while tracking the entry of newer municipalities into the survey framework.
